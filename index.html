Persistence Scholarship Fund

This scholarship supports returning CPE students who are continuing their education while balancing work, family, or other responsibilities. The award is designed to reduce friction for essentials so students can focus on finishing what they started.

Award

$200 - $400

(based on GPA and performance)

Use of funds

Tuition, books, required fees, transit, or general living costs

Disbursement

Monthly while enrolled in a Harper College CPE Program

Eligibility

  • Currently enrolled in A CPE Program at HarperCollege
  • Returning student (continuing after a break, switching programs, or re‑starting toward a credential)
  • In good academic standing with the institution
  • Age 18 or older

How it works

  1. Submit a short application (basic info + a brief statement on how this support will help you persist).
  2. Provide a valid mail.harpercollege.edu email to verify enrollment.
  3. Provide a United States based Phone Number and Paypal ID as funds will be distributed via Paypal
  4. Recipients receive $300 per month for the active term. Disbursements are made electronically.

Selection

Focus on persistence, effort, and clarity of goals rather than GPA alone.

Timeline

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. Awards may begin the same month as approval.

Privacy

Information is used solely to evaluate eligibility and manage disbursements and is not shared or sold.
